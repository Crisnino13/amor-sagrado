What is Holy Love?

Holy Love is a project that integrates history, faith, technology and design. Our aim is to vindicate the love that exists on couples from the LGBTI+ community in relation of the Christian and Catholic tradition through the live of Sergius and Bacchus.

Patrons of love

Based on Catholic art and the work of Jhon Boswell, the main author on the lives of Sergius and Bacchus, we created the first digital sculpture to pay tribute to this two saints, for anyone to download, replicate and share their valuable story.
This open resource is available for printing on any 3D printer.

The holy martyrs

These two Roman noblemen are among the first martyrs canonized and commemorated by the church.
Their love story began at a time when Christianity was persecuted and punished, so they professed their faith in secret. Curiously, the love for their faith was the only thing they had to hide, since their homoaffective relationship was completely accepted.
